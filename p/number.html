How It Works

  1. Multiply your number by 2
  2. Add 10
  3. Divide by 2
  4. Subtract your original number

You will always get 5!

Example

Try 8:

8 → 16 → 26 → 13 → 5

Try It Yourself


Math Proof

Let your number be x:

2x → 2x + 10 → (2x + 10)/2 → x + 5 → 5

Why This Works

  • ×2 and ÷2 cancel each other
  • +10 becomes +5 after dividing
  • Subtracting your number removes it completely

So it secretly becomes: number + 5 − number = 5
